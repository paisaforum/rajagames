  • Set a Budget: Decide a fixed amount to spend on gaming each week and stick to it. Never use money meant for essentials like rent or bills.
  • Limit Play Time: Take regular breaks to avoid excessive gaming. Set a timer to remind yourself to pause.
  • Avoid Chasing Losses: If you lose, don’t increase bets to recover losses. Accept losses as part of the game and play within your limits.
  • Use Self-Exclusion Tools: Contact our 24/7 support to temporarily suspend your account if you need a break.
  • Seek Help if Needed: If gaming feels overwhelming, reach out to professional resources like those listed below.
